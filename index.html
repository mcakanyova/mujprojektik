Mgr. Michaela Čakányová, Ph.D.

I am a teacher and researcher in the fields of syntax, morphology and morphosyntax with a keen interest in pragmatics and philosophy of language. I particularly focus on English and Czech, but I incorporate other languages into my research as well as I aim for cross-linguistic applicability of the theoretical research. My work began in the field of generative grammar slowly navigating from minimalism to more cartographic approach.
